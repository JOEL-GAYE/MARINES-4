Common Poultry Diseases

Disease Name Image Symptoms Prevention
Newcastle Disease Respiratory distress, twisted neck, diarrhea Vaccination, biosecurity measures
Fowl Pox White or yellow lesions on the skin and mouth Vaccination, mosquito control
Coccidiosis Blood in feces, reduced appetite, droopy feathers Sanitation, coccidiostats in feed
  1. Follow the Vaccination schedule properly.
  2. Seperate the sick birds from the healthy ones.
  3. Quality feeds = Healthy birds.
  4. Ensure the henhouse is always clean.
  5. Proper handling of eggs.
